Our Story

Welcome to our website! We are a team of passionate individuals who enjoy discussing films. Our journey began in 2020 when we found each other on a forum. Since then, we have been working tirelessly to create this blog together!. Travelling Cinema believes in the power of every film, regardless of its genre or budget. Our team of devoted writers brings varied perspectives, ensuring a comprehensive evaluation of each movie. From the latest blockbusters to hidden treasures, we strive to cover a broad spectrum of cinematic offerings.
